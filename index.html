US Health & Insurance Data Consolidation System

The US Health & Insurance Data Consolidation System is a comprehensive ETL and analytics project aimed at integrating disparate health and insurance datasets from multiple providers across the United States. The system extracts raw data from sources such as Medicare, Medicaid, commercial claims, and regional hospital databases, transforms it into standardized formats, and loads it into a centralized data warehouse for advanced analysis. The project leverages a full stack of data tools: - ETL Tools: Apache Airflow, Talend, SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) - Data Processing & Analysis: Python (Pandas, NumPy) - Visualization & Reporting: Power BI, Tableau, Matplotlib, Seaborn - Workflow & Version Control: Git, Jira. Key outcomes include interactive dashboards for policy insights, predictive models for claim forecasting, and anomaly detection in billing patterns. The system supports healthcare policy evaluation, cost optimization, and improved accessibility to insurance analytics for stakeholders."
